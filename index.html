For my Aunt Shirlie, with love…
— Nancy
🪶

When I think of you, I think of a beautiful bird—never meant to be caged, always following your own flight.

You have always lived life on your own terms. You traveled where your heart led you, loved deeply, and allowed yourself to be deeply loved in return.

As you know, people often told me, “You’re just like your Aunt Shirlie.” Sometimes they didn’t mean it as a compliment—but I always secretly hoped they were right.

In so many ways, I am your mini me, and I wear that proudly.

I want you to know how much I love you and have always looked up to you for as long as I can remember.

I can’t wait to see you. It’s going to be a long three weeks because I can’t wait to hug your neck.

Love always,
Nancy
